About us

Welcome to LiqRest, where metal whispers stories and every sip becomes an artful celebration. Founded by artist Angela Rica P. Vidal, LiqRest transforms classic automobiles into conversation-starting liquor holders, crafted from ethically sourced recycled metals. Angela's journey began with a whimsical sketch on a napkin, igniting a passion for reimagining metal as something more than cold and rigid. Today, LiqRest's sleek decanters and gravity-defying wine balances grace bar carts across the globe, sparking laughter and elevating gatherings into unforgettable soirees. Join us on this transformative journey where sustainability meets style, and every piece tells a tale. Explore our handcrafted collections, unleash your creativity with bespoke customization, and discover the extraordinary at LiqRest.
